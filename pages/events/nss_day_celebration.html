The National Degree College, Basavanagudi, Bangalore, is organizing an NSS Day Celebration on 30th September 2023 at 10:30 am in the Dr. H.N. Multimedia Hall. The event will be presided over by Prof. Nagesh G N, President, NES Karnataka, and will feature a special address by the NSS Coordinator.

Content:

NSS Day Celebration

  • Date: 30th September 2023
  • Time: 10:30 am
  • Venue: Dr. H.N. Multimedia Hall, The National Degree College, Basavanagudi, Bangalore

Presided by:

  • Prof. Nagesh G N, President, NES Karnataka

Special Address by:

  • NSS Coordinator

Organized by:

  • The National Degree College, Basavanagudi, Bangalore
  • National Service Scheme (NSS) unit of the college

  • "Not Me, But You"

  • To recognize the contributions of NSS volunteers and encourage them to continue their good work
